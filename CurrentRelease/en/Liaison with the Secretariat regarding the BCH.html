Liaison with the Secretariat regarding the BCH
BCH Central Portal Online Help > Information-sharing Obligations On Becoming a Party to the Protocol > Role of the BCH Focal Points > Function of the BCH focal Points > Liaison with the Secretariat regarding the BCH

Glossary Item Box

BCH focal points are responsible for interacting with the CBD Secretariat regarding the technical aspects of national participation in the BCH. This includes providing the Secretariat with feedback on their experiences with the operation of the BCH in order to improve its future development and ability to meet the needs of Parties. The Modalities of Operation of the BCH specifically request focal points to provide advice on further technical development, including suggestions for improvements to the layout and system specifications of the Central Portal and its databases.

Focal points may provide such information directly to the Secretariat at any time or the Secretariat may request feedback through means of, for example, a Notification or questionnaire. In many cases, it will be necessary for the focal point to coordinate responses from a variety of national users in order to provide comprehensive and representative feedback.
