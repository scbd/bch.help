Succession
BCH Central Portal Online Help > Information-sharing Obligations On Becoming a Party to the Protocol > Becoming a Party to the Protocol > Becoming a Party > Succession

Glossary Item Box

Succession occurs when one State replaces another for responsibility over international relations of the State’s territory. Generally, a newly independent State which makes a notification of succession is considered a Party to the Protocol from the date of the succession of the State, or from the date of entry into force of the treaty, whichever is the later date.
