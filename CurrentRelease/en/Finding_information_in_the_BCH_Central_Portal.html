This module will introduce the user to the components of the BCH Central Portal and provide guidance on how to search for information in the BCH databases.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Audience

This module is designed to provide guidance to users of the BCH. It is developed for a non-technical audience with little or no knowledge of the Protocol and the BCH, but with a need to access and use data. Access to information is available to all users, but access to input facilities for the BCH is limited to those who have particular levels of access, e.g. National Focal Points (NFPs) and data entry personnel.

 

Purpose

This module provides detailed information about the databases found within the Finding Information section of the Central Portal. It explains how to find information about the BCH Central Portal. The web-based Central Portal allows governments to administer their information directly in the BCH databases. These databases, which form the nucleus of the BCH, can be searched to find relevant and useful information about the Protocol.

 

Each of the sections will guide the user into the different procedures to retrieve the maximum relevant information available through the BCH Central Portal.
