What is the purpose of the Cartagena Protocol on Biosafety?
BCH Central Portal Online Help > The Cartagena Protocol on Biosafety > What is the purpose of the Cartagena Protocol on Biosafety?

Glossary Item Box

The objective of the Protocol is to contribute to ensuring an adequate level of protection in the field of the safe transfer, handling and use of living modified organisms (LMOs) resulting from modern biotechnology that may have adverse effects on the conservation and sustainable use of biological diversity, taking also into account risks to human health, and focusing, in particular, on transboundary movements. This objective is to be achieved in accordance with the precautionary approach.[1]
