Promoting public awareness and participation (Article 23)
BCH Central Portal Online Help > BCH Help Complete Manuals > Information-sharing Obligations On Becoming a Party to the Protocol > Information to be Shared Following Entry Into Force > Promoting public awareness and participation (Article 23)

Glossary Item Box

The Biosafety Protocol requires and encourages Parties to inform and involve their public in matters relating to LMOs. More specifically, Parties shall:

 

  1. Promote and facilitate public awareness, education and participation concerning the safe transfer, handling and use of LMOs.
  2. Ensure access to information on LMOs.
  3. Consult the public in the decision-making process facilitate public access to the Biosafety Clearing-House.
  4. Endeavour to inform the public about the means of public access to the BCH.
