URL:  http://bch.cbd.int/database/

 

The Compiled lists of information are maintained and regularly updated by the BCH for the following categories of data: National Contacts, LMOs, Organisms and Genes. Unlike information accesible through the search pages, compiled information is provided in a list format, and is easily accessible either on the screen or in a PDF file.

 

Access to the compiled lists of information is provided from the link in the Finding Information drop down menu on the navigation bar or from the link in the left-hand menu of the Finding Information page.
