Training modules

Glossary Item Box

 

                        There are 11 training modules available in PDF format, which provide a range of information from introductory material to in-depth review. Most modules contain interactive exercises and detailed walk-throughs of the steps required for each activity. The following topics are addressed:

                         

• MOD 2 – Introduction to the Biosafety Clearing-House

• MOD 3 – Surfing the BCH Central Portal

• MOD 4 – Finding information in the BCH

• MOD 5 – Recommended Guidelines for Countries

• MOD 6A – Using the Management Centre

• MOD 6B – Registering National Data

• MOD 6C – Sharing Biosafety Information

• MOD 7 – Using the BCH Modules to meet Stakeholder Needs

• MOD 8 – Using the Case Studies

• MOD 9 – Ready Reference Library
