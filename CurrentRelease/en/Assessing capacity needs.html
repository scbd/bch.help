Assessing capacity needs
BCH Central Portal Online Help > BCH Help Complete Manuals > Information-sharing Obligations On Becoming a Party to the Protocol > Information to be Shared Following Entry Into Force > Other requirements > Assessing capacity needs

Glossary Item Box

Most developing countries lack the necessary capacity (e.g. institutional infrastructure, skills and competence in a variety of fields such as risk assessment, risk management, information management etc.,) to be able to implement the Protocol effectively. Accordingly, a necessary first strategic step is to assess and communicate through the BCH their capacity-building needs in order to facilitate needs-driven international cooperation in capacity-building activities (as provided for in Article 22 of the Protocol).
