This module addresses the information-sharing obligations faced by a Party on entry into force of the Protocol. It seeks to explain:

 

  • the information-sharing responsibilities of the BCH National Focal Point;
  • the information that must be made available through the BCH immediately on entry into force of the Protocol;
  • an overview of ongoing information-sharing obligations.

 

 

 

Audience

This module was designed to provide guidance to users of the Biosafety Clearing House (BCH). It was developed particularly for government representatives, including National Focal Points (NFPs).

Purpose

This module examines the information-sharing obligations outlined by the Protocol and relevant decisions by the Conference of the Parties serving as the meeting of the Parties to the Protocol.

It aims also to be an incentive for a non-Party to consider making information available to the BCH even before a country completes its ratification process.

Disclaimer

Please note that this module has been prepared to assist understanding of the Cartagena Protocol on Biosafety. It is not intended to provide legal interpretation of the Protocol or decisions made by the Conference of the Parties serving as the meeting of the Parties to the Protocol. Please refer to the original text of the Protocol and decisions for any further information.
