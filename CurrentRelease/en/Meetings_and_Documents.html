The Meetings and Documents link takes the user to the Calendar of SCBD Meetings page where a list of upcoming meetings related to the Cartagena Protocol on Biosafety can be accessed.

 

The search for meetings and documents can be refined by selecting specific search parameters such as country, year, month and words from the title.
