Glossary Item Box

Common formats are provided to standardize the structure and organization of the information in the BCH databases. Use of these formats improves the efficiency of the BCH Central Portal and ensures easy access to information.

The common formats are the basis of the online entry forms in the Management Centre.

 

 

                                                                     

The Common formats page provides links to MS Word files of the common formats for the following categories of information:

 

1.      National records

  • National Focal Point
  • National Database or Website
  • Competent National Authority
  • Laws and Regulations
  • Country's decisions or any other Communication
  • Risk Assessments
  • Biosafety Expert
  • Report on Biosasfety Expert Assignment

2.      Reference Records

  • Contact Details Reference
  • Capacity-Building Opportunity
  • Capacity-Building Project
  • Academically-Accredited Biosafety Course
  • Biosafety Organization
  • Biosafety Information Resource
  • BCH News
  • Independent Risk Assessments
  • LMO - Unique Identification
  • Inserted Gene
  • Organism (non-modified)
