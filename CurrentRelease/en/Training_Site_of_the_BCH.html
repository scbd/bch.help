Glossary Item Box

The Training Site of the BCH is designed to help BCH users familiarize themselves with the functioning of the Management Center without affecting the official BCH Central Portal.

 

In order to access the Training Site, users require BCH training accounts (which are provided upon request to the Secretariat).

 

 Figure 62

The sign in page provides three additional, useful links for:

  1. Opening a training account
  2. Obtaining a reminder about a password
  3. Access to the official BCH site.
