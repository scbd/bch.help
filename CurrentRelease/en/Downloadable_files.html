Glossary Item Box

The Downloadable Files page provides access to files designed to help Parties fulfill their responsibilities with respect to the Protocol’s information and reporting requirements. It also provides links to guidelines and templates for entering information. The downloadable files are available in MS Word and PDF format in the six official United Nations languages and are grouped as follows:

 

-          Forms

-          Biosafety Documents

-          List of Competent National Authorities and National Focal Point Lists (updated daily)

-          Roster of Experts Reports (updated daily) and summary statistics
