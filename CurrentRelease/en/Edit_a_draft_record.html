Edit a draft record
BCH Central Portal Online Help > BCH Central Portal > Registering Information > The Management Centre main page > Edit a draft record

Glossary Item Box

By clicking on the Edit a draft record link in the left-hand menu, you may edit or delete any existing drafts that you have previously saved.

As with the Edit a published record function, you can filter the record types in order to quickly identify which records you want to work on. Please note that Draft National Records saved here may be accessed by the BCH-NFP and any National Authorized User for editing and/or publication.
