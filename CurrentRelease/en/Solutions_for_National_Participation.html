Glossary Item Box

The Solutions for National Participation links take the user to a page which provides technical information (and links to the manuals) on Hermes and the BCH Ajax Plug-In. 

 

Both of these solutions are aimed toward improving the ease of accessing information in the BCH in response to identified needs of users. They were created to assist countries developing their BCH national nodes to be interlinked and interoperable with the Central Portal.
