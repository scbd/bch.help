The “Sign in” mechanism
BCH Central Portal Online Help > BCH Central Portal > Registering Information > The “Sign in to the Biosafety Clearing-House” page > The “Sign in” mechanism

Glossary Item Box

In order to access the Registering Information section, you need to sign in with your registered BCH account.

To sign in: (i) enter the email address which you used to register for a BCH account in the Registered email address field, (ii) enter the password contained in the automatic message you received when you registered in the Password field and (iii) click the Sign In button.

If you select the Remember me checkbox, the computer you are using will remember your email address (but not the password) the next time you sign in to the Management Centre.
